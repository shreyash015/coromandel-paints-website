About Us

Pioneering Innovation inPaint Manufacturing

Founded in 1987, Coromandel Paints Industries has grown from a small family business into one of the region's most respected paint manufacturers. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has made us the preferred choice for architects, builders, and homeowners alike.

With three state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a dedicated team of chemists and engineers, we produce over 50 million liters of premium paints annually, serving markets across 45 countries.
